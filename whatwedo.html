Real Estate

Horizon has been developing residential, commercial and hospitality real estate for more than thirty years. Current assets include The Mukuba Mall, Kitwe and The Royal Solwezi Hotel, Solwezi.

Royal Solwezi Hotel

The Royal Solwezi is a luxury, 48-bedroom, 14-apartment hotel and conference centre located close to the Kansanshi Mine, east of the town centre. Including suites, double and single rooms, plus one and two-bedroom self-contained apartments, the hotel is suitable for both short and long-stay guests. There is an on-site bar and restaurant open to residents and non-residents.

Mukuba Mall

Opened in 2015, Mukuba Mall in Kitwe is one of the largest shopping centres in Zambia’s Copperbelt with a gross land area in excess of 28,000m2. The mall includes anchor tenants Shoprite, Game and Pink n Pay. Horizon has a 50% share in the development.

Project Development

Horizon’s project development activities are focused on natural resources in infrastructure, including power generation. Horizon works with local partners to identify and source high priority development targets.

Natural Resources

Horizon’s flagship project is the Kitwe / Uchi Tailings Retreatment project located on the Copperbelt in Zambia. The project comprises three tailings licences with a total area of 250 hectares located within the Kitwe City Limits. The project is a Joint Venture Agreement with ZCCM-IH, Zambia’s state mining company. Comprising approximately 100 million tonnes of material, Horizon will recover more than 180,000 tonnes of copper and 12,000 tonnes of cobalt. An independent bank-feasibility study has been completed and Horizon will be seeking finance partners to fund the construction and commissioning of the processing plant. An additional 180 million tonnes of tailings located within 50km of Kitwe has been acquired with test work and process design in the early stages.

Power Generation

Horizon is developing both traditional base-load and renewable power generation projects aimed at providing low-cost and reliable power within Zambia. A coal resource has been secured to supply a 300MW thermal power station providing base-load power to key commercial customers.

Private Equity

Horizon Private Equity partners with management teams to accelerate business growth. Investment ranges from seed capital for scalable, technology driven businesses, to development capital for companies moving to the next stage of development.

Corporate and Social Responsibility

Central to our investment and project development activities is our determination to support economic growth alongside a positive environmental impact. Our projects create employment for local labour, providing secure jobs, training and development, as well as additional opportunities for our local supply-chain. Minimising the impact to the environment is a key objective of all projects. Horizon will make a major contribution to the clean-up of a number of towns and cities through the removal and retreatment of historic mine waste. Horizon is determined to make a positive contribution to the communities where it is established in addition to complying with all applicable laws, regulations and ethical standards.
