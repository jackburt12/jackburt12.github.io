  • Bradley Rawson
    Chief Executive Officer

    Bradley’s background is in Investment Banking in London, with Nomura International PLC. Prior to the forming of Horizon Mining Ltd, Bradley was CFO at Horizon Corporation Ltd overseeing all financial aspects of the company’s investment portfolio and has led their Southern African portfolio since 2001.

  • Grant Rawson
    Chief Operating Officer

    Grant Rawson has been employed by Horizon Corporation since 2001, and has played an integral role in overseeing the development and management of our African projects. With knowledge of the local culture, local connections, and his understanding of the unique challenges experienced in the various aspects of the local communities throughout Zambia, he has been a solid asset in securing and expanding our African assets

  • Charlie Chase-Gardener
    Project Development Director

    Charlie sits in London and works on the financing requirements of both Horizon Corporation Ltd and Horizon Mining. His experience is in private equity and asset management sectors and has been involved in financing transactions across a range of industries and jurisdictions.

  • Darius Ghesmati
    Project Development Director

    Darius holds an MSc in global banking and finance from the European Business School. Darius has worked on trading desks at a number of investment banks and has a strong understanding of both financial and commodity markets

  • Simon Burt
    Chief Financial Officer

    Simon has more than 20 years experience as the senior finance executive for both listed and privately owned businesses ranging in size from start up to annual revenues of more than £400m. Simon has extensive fundraising experience having managed capital raising projects with combined funding of in excess of £1bn, from both private funds and public markets. Simon has excellent M&A experience having completed some 20 acquisitions and disposals, including public to private transactions. Simon qualified as a Chartered Accountant with KPMG.
